 Loyalty KPI Dashboard Analysis.

Everyday Rewards - Sydney Stores (2023-2024).

 Project Overview

This project offers a detailed discussion of the customer loyalty performance within the Woolworths stores in Sydney based on the interactive power BI Loyalty KPI Dashboard. The dashboard will assess membership expansion, customer purchasing behavior, reward point behavior and store level performance to create business actionable insights.

 Key Objectives

Examine customer loyalty interaction.

Track points collected divided by the number of points that are redeemed over time.

Determine the successful places of stores.

Assess status distribution of membership.

Retail strategy Support decision-making based on data.

 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
 Membership Growth

1,000 members registered in CY 2023

Registration trend indicates constant growth but seasonally.

Most of the members are active and this implies that the program is high in terms of retention.

 Transaction Activity

Total transactions till EOFY 2024: 5.5K.

Good transaction volume indicates constant customer interaction.

 Rewards Performance

Points Earned: 874K

Points Redeemed: 42K

Gap demonstrates good accumulation habits with a chance to enhance the redemption participation.

 Revenue Impact

Value of total transaction: AUD 876.14K.

Shows great commercial value of loyalty members.

 Business Recommendations and Insights.

 Boost redemption awareness with personal offers.
 Introduce store-based campaigns in those locations that are not doing well.
 Promote the use of points in enhancing customer satisfaction.
 Use those stores that perform excellently as models.
